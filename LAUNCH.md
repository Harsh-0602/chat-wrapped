# 24-Hour Launch Plan

Virality cannot be guaranteed. The goal is to maximise completed analyses and
shares without spending money.

## Positioning

**Hook:** Find out who carries your WhatsApp chat. Private, free, and processed
entirely on your phone.

Do not lead with "AI". Lead with curiosity and privacy.

## Before launch

- Test one Android export, one iPhone export, and one group export.
- Confirm the share card contains the live URL.
- Record a 12-second vertical screen capture: export, analyse, reveal, share.
- Prepare three cards using fictional/demo chats.

## Launch sequence (India time)

### 7:30 PM

Post the short video on Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts:

> I analysed 30,000 WhatsApp messages without uploading them anywhere.
> Turns out I start 73% of our conversations. Try yours free.

### 7:40 PM

Send it to 10 close friends individually. Ask them to try it and share only
their result card, not a promotional message.

### 8:00 PM

Post in relevant college, hostel, friend-group, and creator WhatsApp/Telegram
communities where promotion is allowed.

### 8:30 PM

Publish on LinkedIn/X with the privacy engineering angle:

> Built a WhatsApp Wrapped that never uploads your chat. No login, backend,
> analytics, or AI API. The whole app runs in your browser and is open source.

### 9:00 PM

Post to relevant Reddit communities only after checking their self-promotion
rules. Use a transparent "I built this" title and answer technical/privacy
questions directly.

### Next morning

Post one surprising aggregate-free demo insight, reply to every comment, and
fix parsing failures immediately. Never collect or publish user chat data.

## Share loop

The free result must remain useful. Each card includes:

- "Make yours free"
- Product URL
- No message contents
- Shortened participant names

## Success metrics

Measure manually for the first launch:

- People who report completing an analysis
- Result cards shared or tagged
- Parsing failures by export format
- GitHub stars/forks

Do not add invasive analytics merely to produce a vanity visitor count.
