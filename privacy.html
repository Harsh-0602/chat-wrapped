WChat Wrapped
Plain-English privacy

Your chat stays yours.

Chat Wrapped processes exported WhatsApp text entirely inside your browser.

What we collect

Nothing. There is no account, database, analytics tracker, advertising script, or server upload in this version.

What happens to your chat

Your browser reads the selected file, calculates statistics, and creates a result card locally. Closing or refreshing the page clears the analysis.

Network requests

The launch build uses no external fonts, APIs, images, or third-party scripts. GitHub Pages may retain standard infrastructure logs when serving the static files.

Important

Only analyse chats you are allowed to access. Avoid publicly sharing cards that reveal private names or sensitive information.
